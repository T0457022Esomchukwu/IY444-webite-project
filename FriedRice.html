Nigerian Fried Rice

  • Prep time:30 mins
  • Cooking time:1 hr
  • Calories ~ 2989kcal

Ingredients:


  • 1kg long grain rice ~ 1370kcal
  • 200 g Shrimps or Prawns ~ 200 kcal
  • 300 g Mixed veggies or carrot, green peas and green beans (for freshness) ~ 150kcal
  • 1 clove of Garlic or 1 tsp of garlic paste ~ 4kcal
  • 2 onions ~ 80kcal
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers ~ 10kcal
  • 1inch of ginger ~ 2kcal
  • 2 chicken breasts ~ 370kcal
  • 2 tsp Curry powder ~ 12kcal
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper ~ 12kcal
  • 2 tsp thyme ~ 2kcal
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cubes of Maggi seasoning ~ 12kcal
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper ~ 6kcal
  • 1 tsp white pepper ~ 7kcal
  • 1 tsp chilli pepper ~ 6 kcal
  • 6 tbsp vegetable oil ~ 744kcal
  • 2 tsp salt ~ 0kcal
