Chicken Shawarma

  • Prep time:2hrs mins
  • Cooking time:20 mins
  • Calories ~ 961kcal

Ingredients:


  • ¼ Cup of Greek yogurt~36kcal
  • 1 Chicken Breast ~ 185kcal
  • 2 Tortilla/Pita (preferably pita) ~350 kcal
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice~3kcal
  • 1 tbsp sugar~16kcal
  • 1 tsp. Salt~0kcal
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper~6kcal
  • 1 tsp cumin~8kcal
  • 2 tsp paprika~ 12kcal
  • 1 tsp black pepper~6kcal
  • 1 tsp Garlic Paste or 1 clove of garlic~4kcal
  • ¼ cup mayo ~ 229 kcal
  • 100 g cabbage ~ 25kcal
  • 3 tbsp ketchup ~ 51kcal
  • 1 Carrot ~ 30kcal

Instructions


  1. Dice the chicken breast into chunks and put it in a bowl.
  2. Add 1 tsp salt, 1tsp cayenne pepper, 1tsp cumin, 1tsp paprika,1tsp garlic paste,1tsp black pepper and mix.
  3. Put it in the fridge to let it marinate for about an 1hr 45 mins.
  4. After marinating put it in the air fryer or fry on a pan for 12 mins.
  5. Finely chop cabbage and grate the carrot.
  6. In a bowl put 1tsp paprika, ¼ cup mayo,3 tbsp ketchup,1tsp sugar and 1tbsp lemon juice and mix.
  7. In a bowl add the sauce, cabbage, carrot, chicken and mix.
  8. place the tortilla on a plate and put the filling in the middle.
  9. Fold the end the sides of the tortilla and roll it up.
  10. Place the folded side of shawarma on a pan on high heat and flip when it is charred.
