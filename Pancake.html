Pancake & Corned beef sauce

  • Prep time:10 mins
  • Cooking time:30 mins
  • Calories ~ 1780kcal

Ingredients


  • 200 g Corned Beef~500 kcal
  • 1 Spring Onion~2kcal
  • 100 g Cabbage ~25 kcal
  • 1 Onions~40 kcal
  • 1 Scotch Bonnet~5kcal
  • 1 Carrot~30 kcal
  • 1 cup of Flour ~455 kcal
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil~248 kcal
  • 1 Egg~60kcal
  • 1¼ cups of Skimmed Milk~106kcal
  • 1 tbsp Sugar~48kcal
  • 1 tsp Salt~0kcal
  • 1 tsp black pepper~6kcal
  • 1 tsp white pepper~7kcal
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil~248kcal

Instructions


Corned beef sauce


  1. Finely chop cabbage, spring onions, onions, scotch bonnet and carrot.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan on medium heat and fry onions, scotch bonnet and white ends of spring onion for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the 100g Cabbage and corned beef and cook for 3 minutes (If it is too dry add a little bit of water).
  4. Add1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp white pepper, 1 Maggi cube and 1 tsp salt and fry for 1 minute.
  5. Then add in the carrots and the green ends of spring onions and fry 2 minutes.

Pancakes


  1. In a bowl add 1 cup of flour, 1 tbsp sugar and a pinch of salt and mix.
  2. Then add 2 tbsp oil ,1 egg and 1¼ cups of Milk then mix.
  3. In a pan medium heat add cooking spray or a little bit of oil.
  4. Using a ladle pour a full spoon of the batter on the pan and spread to be as thin as possible.
  5. When the top of the crepe is dry , Flip it.
  6. Place the pancake on a plate and place a line of corned beef through the middle.
  7. Place line of corned beef sauce through the middle and roll it up.
