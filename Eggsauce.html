Egg Sauce

  • Prep time:5 mins
  • Cooking time:10 mins
  • Calories ~ 257kcal

Ingredients:


  • 1 tomato~22kcal
  • ½ Onion~20kcal
  • 1 scotch bonnet~5kcal
  • 2 eggs~120kcal
  • Cooking spray or 2 teaspoons of Vegetable Oil~80kcal
  • ½ tsp of salt~0kcal
  • 1 Maggi cube~6kcal
  • 1 tsp of thyme~1kcal
  • 1 tsp of chilli pepper~3kcal

Instructions:


  1. Finely chop your tomato, onion and scotch bonnet pepper.
  2. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl and whisk until it fully combined.
  3. Heat the frying pan with medium heat until its hot and then add the oil or cooking spray.
  4. Add your onions and fry for 1-2 minutes
  5. Add your Tomatoes and fry for 1-2 minutes
  6. Add your scotch bonnet and fry for 1-2 minutes, if it is too dry add a little bit of water and let it cook.
  7. Add your spices, then add your beaten eggs
  8. Scramble the eggs until they reach your required consistency.
